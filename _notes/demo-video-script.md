# Trovar — Home page demo video script (~75s)

Goal: an NZ accountant instantly gets "this collects every supplier invoice my
clients spend on, automatically, and it's audit-ready." StoryBrand: the
accountant is the hero, Trovar is the guide that removes the chasing.

Format recommendation: AI intro (Higgsfield) → real screen recording of the app
→ AI outro/CTA. The middle (real screens) is what sells it.

---

## SCENE 1 — Hook (0:00–0:08)  [AI b-roll or real desk shot]
ON SCREEN: A messy inbox / pile of supplier statements, then it dissolves into the clean Trovar dashboard.
VO: "Every month, the same hunt — chasing down your clients' invoices from a dozen different platforms."

## SCENE 2 — The promise (0:08–0:16)  [Trovar dashboard, real screen]
ON SCREEN: Trovar Overview — "Total captured", recent invoices list with Meta/Google logos.
VO: "Trovar collects them for you. Every supplier invoice, every paid subscription your clients run — in one place."

## SCENE 3 — Connect once (0:16–0:30)  [real screen: client page → connect]
ON SCREEN: Open a client → click Connect on Meta Ads → OAuth consent → "Connected".
VO: "Connect a client's accounts once — securely, through the platform's own login. No passwords stored, and it never breaks when they add 2FA."

## SCENE 4 — It just appears (0:30–0:42)  [real screen: invoices page]
ON SCREEN: Invoices page — month range, invoices listed with amounts, GST, PDF buttons; the spend chart.
VO: "From then on, invoices land automatically — supplier, date, amount and GST captured, ready to reconcile in Xero."

## SCENE 5 — Catch everything (0:42–0:54)  [real screen: forward email + manual add]
ON SCREEN: Forwarding address copied; an email forwarded; the invoice appears. Then "Add invoice" manual entry.
VO: "Anything off-platform? Forward it to the client's Trovar address, or add it by hand. Nothing slips through."

## SCENE 6 — The team does the connecting (0:54–1:04)  [real screen: connect link]
ON SCREEN: Copy the client connect link → the branded public connect page on a phone → client's staff tap Connect.
VO: "You can't log in to all their accounts — so send the client a link, and their team connects their own. Done in two minutes."

## SCENE 7 — Close / CTA (1:04–1:15)  [AI outro, brand]
ON SCREEN: Trovar logo, "First client free, forever", Get started button.
VO: "Stop chasing invoices. Start with one client, free, forever. Trovar — built and supported right here in New Zealand."

---

different platforms. Trovar collects them for you: every supplier invoice, every
paid subscription your clients run, in one place. Connect a client's accounts

nothing slips through. And because you can't log in to all their accounts, you
send the client a link and their own team connects in two minutes. Stop chasing
invoices. Start with one client, free, forever. Trovar — built and supported
right here in New Zealand.
